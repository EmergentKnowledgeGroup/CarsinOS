## Closure protocol

Version 1.1 supersedes version 1.0's financial plumbing, blanket category approval floors, deny-wins authority model, and destructive-action hard locks. Closing a release blocker requires the exact evidence path, command/result, source head, reviewer, and linked checklist IDs. A fallback may reduce exposed capability for a technical proof gap but may not become a permanent owner-action prohibition or convert missing proof to PASS. Reopening a blocker is mandatory when later evidence or amended product behavior invalidates closure.
